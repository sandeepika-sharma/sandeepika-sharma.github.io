Dr. Sandeepika Sharma

I am a senior engineer at Qualcomm, where I work on 5G, wireless communication.

Prior to joining Qualcomm, I was Ph.D. candidate at National Institute of Technology, Krukshetra. I was working under the supervision of Prof. Brahmjit Singh. I did Masters in Mobile and Pervasive Computing and Bachelors in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Indira Gandhi Delhi Technological UNiversity for Women, Delhi.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Github

Research Interests

Future wireless network, Resource allocation, Physical Layer Communication, Power management and Vehicular-to-Vehicular Communication.
